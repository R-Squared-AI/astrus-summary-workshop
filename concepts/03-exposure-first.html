Concept 03 — "Exposure-First"

Thesis: Lead with the money. The three headline exposures are the biggest figures on the card; one honest stacked bar shows how the insured dollar exposure is composed (revenue vs payroll — vehicle count is shown separately because you can't add vehicles to dollars). Each figure and each line of business carries its own Produced status, and the loss-run "Not produced" flag pops in red.

Underwriter job
"Show me the exposures first — did we get GL revenue, WC payroll, the auto count — and is anything missing?"
Status semantics
Green = LOB present & its expected exposure produced. Amber = present but a specific field empty (Excess has no effective date). Red = requested, nothing produced (loss runs). No completeness is invented.
