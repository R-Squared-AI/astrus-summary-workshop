About this concept
Thesis
Priority Signal Cards — the underwriter's ranked checklist as a curated stack of signal cards in emphasis order. Signal 1 is biggest, later signals grow quieter; the ordering itself is the information design.
Underwriter job
In two seconds: who is this, which lines came back, are the exposures there, and — the flag Rich wants first — did we get a loss run. Statuses use only PRODUCED / PARTIAL / NOT PRODUCED, each mapped to a real field, defined in the persistent legend.
LWC feasibility
c-astrus-priority-signals: stacked lightning-card + custom signal cards over LDS getRecord(Astrus_Submission__c) and one Apex getter for the deterministic PRODUCED rows; lightning-icon / -badge / -formatted-currency; left accent rail and the CA/HI vehicle micro-viz are inline CSS/SVG. Light mode, no external libs/fonts, LWS-safe.
