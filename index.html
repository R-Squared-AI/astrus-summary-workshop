Astrus Submission Summary

Round 4 converges on the rows direction (Round-3 Concept 02) with Tyler's fixes: bolder red / green / yellow status, much less description, fields custom to each line of business (Commercial Auto shows its vehicle schedule + count, not loss history), and Clearance folded in as just another uniform row. Three close treatments of the same card so you can keep filtering. Buildable as a light-mode Lightning Web Component at the top of the Quality tab.

Shared sample submission — every concept renders this

A & B Painting, Inc. · SUB 022667 3 named insureds · New · Clearance Requested CA + HI · Broker R2 Solutions · Chubb · TPA ESIS WC payroll $4,928,350 GL revenue $8,500,000 (incl. $1.05M sub) Commercial Auto — CA 31 · HI 2 power units Excess Liability — Partial (no effective date) WC & GL loss runs — Missing

Each concept is a single card of uniform rows — one per line of business, plus a Clearance row. Each row shows only the fields that matter to that line, as bold red / green / yellow status: Workers' Comp shows payroll + loss runs; GL shows revenue/sales + loss runs; Commercial Auto shows its vehicle schedule + count and loss runs; Excess shows the field holding it back. The per-line fields are now grounded in the live QA data model — e.g., loss history was put back on Commercial Auto because the data shows 1,146 loss-history summaries attach to Auto lines, refuting the earlier "auto has no loss history" guess. The two state carve-outs — Hawaii Commercial Auto and Connecticut GL — appear only when relevant; on this submission HI Auto shows (2 units) and CT GL does not. Standardized status, no freeform AI prose.

Present — produced; the value is shown.
Partial — produced, but one expected field is empty (no effective date on Excess).
Missing — requested but nothing came back (WC & GL loss runs).
Carve-outs — Hawaii Auto & Connecticut GL show only when relevant.

Round 1 — superseded

The original six concepts. Round-2 feedback removed dark mode, extraction-confidence, reprocessing controls, and tabs — so these are archived, not featured.

These are static HTML/CSS mockups of a future Lightning Web Component (c-astrus-submission-summary) pinned to the top of the Quality tab — hand-rolled to match SLDS tokens, type ladder, and status semantics, with no external frameworks, fonts, or CDNs.
