Design Round 2 · 6 New Concepts

Astrus Submission Summary

Six new design concepts for the at-a-glance summary pinned to the top of an Astrus insurance submission in Salesforce — a beautiful, highly informative snapshot of what the submission contains and which lines of business were produced. Every concept renders the same live sample submission so they compare head-to-head, and each is realistically buildable as a light-mode Lightning Web Component.

Shared sample submission — every concept renders this

A & B Painting, Inc. · SUB 022667 3 named insureds · New · Artisan Contractor Term 07/01/2026 – 07/01/2027 CA + HI · Broker R2 Solutions · Chubb WC payroll $4,928,350 GL revenue $8,500,000 Commercial Auto 33 vehicles Excess Liability — Partial Loss runs — Not produced

This gallery presents candidate designs for a single high-signal panel that answers an underwriter's first questions in seconds: who is the applicant, which lines of business were produced, what are the key exposures, and are the loss runs here? The design centers on the priorities underwriters actually named — named insured first, then lines of business (“boom, boom, boom”) with a crisp per-LOB PRODUCED status, then the exposure inside each LOB, then the loss-run flag.

Produced — the LOB is present and its expected exposure was produced (WC has payroll, GL has revenue, Auto has a vehicle schedule).
Partial — the LOB was produced, but a specific expected element is verifiably empty (e.g. no effective date).
Not produced — the LOB is on the submission but no data was produced for it. Requested, but nothing came back.
