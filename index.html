Design Round 3 · 6 close variations

Astrus Submission Summary

Six close variations on one idea from the Jul-13 Underwriter-Controlled Reprocessing workshop: a standardized card pinned to the top of the Quality tab that an underwriter reads in one glance — line of business by line of business, are the exposures here (how many, how much) and are the loss runs here — plus the clearance essentials (revenue, NAICS, SIC). Same submission, same data, same structure in all six; they differ only in layout, so you can filter to the treatment that feels right. Each is buildable as a light-mode Lightning Web Component.

Shared sample submission — every concept renders this

A & B Painting, Inc. · SUB 022667 3 named insureds · New · Clearance Requested CA + HI · Broker R2 Solutions · Chubb · TPA ESIS WC payroll $4,928,350 GL revenue $8,500,000 (incl. $1.05M sub) Commercial Auto — CA 31 · HI 2 power units Excess Liability — Partial (no effective date) Loss runs — Not produced

Every concept answers the underwriter's first questions before they open the file: which lines of business came in, is each line's exposure present (how many, and what's the total), and are the loss runs here? The organizing unit is the line of business — under each one, sub-tiles give a present / count / total read for exposure and for loss history. A clearance tile carries the rest of what's needed to clear (revenue, NAICS, SIC, effective date, primary state). The two state carve-outs — Hawaii Commercial Auto and Connecticut GL — appear only when relevant; on this submission HI Auto shows (2 Hawaii units) and CT GL does not. Standardized checkbox-style status, no freeform AI prose — so the card reads the same way every time. All six are one card (not a dashboard), light mode only.

Present — the item was produced; the value is shown (payroll, revenue, a vehicle count, a code).
Partial — produced, but one expected field is verifiably empty (here, no effective date on Excess).
Not produced — requested on the submission but nothing came back (here, every line's loss runs).
Carve-outs — Hawaii Auto & Connecticut GL show only when relevant.

The six concepts

Round 2 — superseded

The six round-2 concepts, kept for reference. Feedback from the workshop: they were over-designed dashboards; the brief is a standardized, at-a-glance checklist of what came in — so round 3 rebuilds around per-LOB exposure + loss-history sub-tiles and a clearance tile.

Round 1 — superseded

The original six concepts. Round-2 feedback removed dark mode, extraction-confidence, reprocessing controls, and tabs — so these are archived, not featured.

These are static HTML/CSS mockups of a future Lightning Web Component (c-astrus-submission-summary) pinned to the top of the Quality tab — hand-rolled to match SLDS tokens, type ladder, and status semantics, with no external frameworks, fonts, or CDNs. Data comes from getRecord + an Apex getSummary() returning per-LOB exposure/loss rows and clearance items. Light mode only. Sample data reflects the alliantinsurance--qa record SUB 022667.
